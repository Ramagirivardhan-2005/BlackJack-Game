Blackjack Game Rules:

  1. Winning Condition:
    • You win if the total value of your cards equals exactly 21.
  2. Taking Additional Cards:
    • If your score is below 21, you may continue taking additional cards to improve your score.
  3. Card Dropping and Drawing:
    • If your score exceeds 21, you can drop one card and draw a new one. You have up to 5 chances to do this.
  4. Game Progression:
    • You may continue drawing cards until you either win, lose, or choose to stop the game.

How to Play Game?

  1. To start the game, press the "Start Game" button.
  2. If your score is less than 21, a "Next Card" button will appear, allowing you to draw additional cards.
  3. The "Stop" button allows you to pause the game. If you want to start a new game, it will prompt you for confirmation before resetting.
  4. The"Drop"button allows you to drop a card
  5. The "Restart" button will start a fresh game..go back->
